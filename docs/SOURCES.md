## üî∂ CONTENT REJECTION CRITERIA

### When to Respond: "This is not Factually correct."

Content must be rejected if:

1. **No Source Available**
   - Cannot be verified from trusted sources
   - Only found in unreliable online forums
   - Contradicts established scholarship

2. **Copyright Violation**
   - Full text of copyrighted sutras
   - Modern translations under copyright
   - Proprietary ritual guides

3. **Historical Uncertainty**
   - Dates that cannot be verified
   - Events lacking historical evidence
   - Claims contradicting scholarship

4. **Invented Content**
   - New mantras or sutras
   - Fabricated historical events
   - Unverified Panchang timings

5. **Sect Confusion**
   - Mixing ≈övetƒÅmbara and Digambara beliefs without distinction
   - Presenting one sect's view as universal
   - Ignoring documented differences
